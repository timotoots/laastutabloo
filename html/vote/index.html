  • About

    Laastutabloo (Shingle display) is a installation in Estonian National Museum.

    It displays real-time information from all kinds of online information sources.

    To use is this app, you should be in the museum to interact with the display.

    To create your own display, use our online version.

  • Data providers
    • Police and Border Guard Board
      Politsei- ja Piirivalveamet

      Includes registries of incidents.
  • Help

    To use is this app, you should be visiting Estonian National Museum in Tartu!

    You can vote for your favourite districts in Estonia to be shown on the display. You can choose between all Estonian villages, towns or cities.

    Voting
    Choose "Vote for district" in the menu and find your favourite district.

    Search
    You can also search for district by name. Type in the search box below.

    Playlist
    The playlist of the exhibit is created by votes of the visitors. The district that get more votes will be shown next. Open "View playlist" to see the queue. Ask a friend to vote to bring your favourite up!

  • Create your own display

    Laastutabloo online version.

    You can use online version of Laastutabloo to create your own display. You can customize the districts and data sources you are interested in.
